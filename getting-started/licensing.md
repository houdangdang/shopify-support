# Theme Licensing and Transfers

Information about theme licenses and how to transfer them.

## License Types

### Single Store License
- Valid for one Shopify store
- Includes all future updates
- Includes standard support

### Unlimited License
- Valid for unlimited stores
- Ideal for agencies and developers
- Priority support included

## License Activation

Your theme is automatically licensed when purchased through:
- Shopify Theme Store
- Verve website

::: info License Key
No license key is required. Your purchase is linked to your Shopify account.
:::

## Theme Transfers

### Within Your Account
You can freely move your theme between your own stores.

### To Another Account
To transfer a theme license:

1. Contact our [support team](/contact)
2. Provide proof of purchase
3. Provide the new account details
4. We'll process the transfer within 24-48 hours

::: warning Transfer Limitations
- Each license can only be transferred once
- Original purchaser must initiate the request
- Custom code modifications are not transferred
:::

## Refund Policy

- **14-day refund** period from purchase
- Theme must not have been extensively customized
- Contact Shopify for Theme Store purchases
