# Footer Configuration

Customize your store's footer.

## Footer Layout

Choose your footer style:

| Layout | Columns |
|--------|---------|
| Simple | 1 column centered |
| Standard | 3-4 columns |
| Expanded | 4-5 columns |

## Footer Sections

### Navigation
Add footer menus:
1. Go to **Online Store** → **Navigation**
2. Create footer menu
3. Link in footer settings

### Newsletter
Enable email signup:
- Custom heading
- Placeholder text
- Success message
- Link to Shopify Email or app

### Social Links
Configure in **Theme settings** → **Social media**:
- Instagram
- Facebook
- Twitter/X
- TikTok
- YouTube
- Pinterest

### Contact Information
Display store details:
- Address
- Phone number
- Email
- Business hours

## Payment Icons

Show accepted payment methods:
1. Enable **Show payment icons**
2. Icons auto-populate from Shopify Payments

## Legal Links

Add bottom footer links:
- Privacy policy
- Terms of service
- Refund policy
- Shipping policy

## Copyright

Customize copyright text:
- Store name
- Year (auto-updates)
- Custom message
